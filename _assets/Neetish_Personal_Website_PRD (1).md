
# PRD: Personal Branding Website – Neetish

## 1. Overview
This document defines the product requirements for Neetish’s personal branding website.

The site positions Neetish as an AI Product Expert and Consultant, showcases hands-on projects, captures ongoing thinking, and encourages open conversations around AI.

The website should feel:
- Curious
- Collaborative
- Practical
- Builder-led
- Lightweight and fast

Primary goal: Start conversations around AI workshops, audits, MVP development, and automation projects.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- Startup founders exploring AI ideas
- SaaS teams adding AI features
- Business leaders seeking AI clarity
- Teams looking for AI workshops or audits
- Curious builders and practitioners

---

## 3. Success Metrics
- Contact form submissions
- Direct outreach via LinkedIn or email
- Time spent on Product Lab and Thought Journal
- Returning visitors
- GitHub and social link clicks

---

## 4. Site Structure
1. Home (includes Live Feed)
2. Services
3. Product Lab (Portfolio)
4. Thought Journal
5. Contact

*No separate Live Feed page*

---

## 5. Home Page

### 5.1 Purpose
Introduce Neetish, establish credibility, and show ongoing activity.

### 5.2 Core Sections
- Hero section with positioning and soft CTA
- Short personal introduction
- Embedded Live Feed showing recent activity
- Navigation to Services, Product Lab, and Thought Journal

---

## 6. Embedded Live Feed (Home Page)

### 6.1 Purpose
- Make the site feel active and current
- Showcase curiosity and continuous building

### 6.2 Feed Items
- New Product Lab projects
- Thought Journal posts
- GitHub activity (commits, repos)
- Experiments in progress
- Workshop announcements

### 6.3 Display
- Compact vertical or card-based feed
- Lightweight and subtle
- Auto-updating where possible
- Visible but not dominant on the Home page

---

## 7. Services Page

### 7.1 Services Offered
1. AI Workshops  
2. AI Audits  
3. AI MVP Consulting  
4. AI Automation Projects  

### 7.2 Tone
- Practical
- Outcome-driven
- Conversational
- Clear and jargon-free

---

## 8. Product Lab (Portfolio Section)

### 8.1 Purpose
Showcase hands-on work and experimentation to establish credibility and depth.

### 8.2 Section Naming
Working name: Product Lab  
(Final name may vary: AI Studio, Build Log, Idea Kitchen, Neetish Labs)

### 8.3 Core Features
- Add unlimited projects
- Each project includes:
  - Title
  - Short summary
  - Detailed description (Problem, Approach, Outcome)
  - Category (MVP, Automation, Experiment, Client Work)
  - Status (Live, Prototype, Experiment, Archived)
  - Screenshots (multiple)
  - Deployment link (if available)
  - GitHub link (if available)
  - Date added

---

### 8.4 Project Listing View
- Grid or card-based layout
- Displays:
  - Project title
  - One-line description
  - Category tag
  - Status badge

---

### 8.5 Project Detail Page
- Full project description
- Screenshot gallery / carousel
- External links
- Clear CTA:
  “Interested in something like this? Let’s discuss.”

---

### 8.6 Content Management
- Easy project creation and updates
- Markdown or no-code friendly
- Scalable structure for future growth

---

## 9. Thought Journal

### 9.1 Purpose
Capture evolving ideas, learnings, and experiments.

### 9.2 Content Types
- Short notes
- Frameworks
- AI product breakdowns
- Tool experiments
- Learnings from builds

### 9.3 Features
- Markdown-based posts
- Chronological ordering
- Tag support
- Optional featured posts
- Digital-garden style (not polished blog)

---

## 10. Contact Page

### 10.1 Purpose
Encourage conversation rather than hard conversion.

### 10.2 Content
- Friendly CTA encouraging discussion
- Contact form:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Message
- Social links:
  - LinkedIn
  - GitHub
  - Email
  - Twitter/X (optional)

### 10.3 Tone
Open, warm, low-pressure.

---

## 11. Non-Functional Requirements
- Fast load times
- Mobile responsiveness
- SEO optimized
- Clean URLs
- Easy content updates
- Secure contact forms
- Minimal animations

---

## 12. Out of Scope (Current Phase)
- Payments
- User authentication
- Newsletter subscriptions
- Analytics dashboards
- Community features

---

## 13. Future Enhancements
- Booking calendar integration
- Newsletter
- Workshop-specific landing pages
- Deep-dive case studies
- Speaking engagements section

---

## 14. Tech & Build Preferences
- Static or hybrid rendering
- Markdown-first content
- Easy deployment
- Compatible with AI-first builders (e.g., Antigravity)

---

## 15. Summary
This website is a living, evolving space — part portfolio, part thinking journal, part conversation starter.

It should grow naturally as Neetish continues building, learning, and collaborating in the AI and product space.
